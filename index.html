RMS Ltd.

Ruari Mears Solutions · worker-owned tech cooperative in formation

We develop software and we're building our own AI compute hardware. Every employee will own a stake. Every salary will be set democratically by the team — including the founder's.

Where we are

RMS Ltd. is incorporated and not trading. There is no trading finance structure yet — accounts and cooperative governance in full will follow when we start trading.

Right now the company is founder-led: one employee in practice, building the portfolio before we hire. We are applying the worker-owned cooperative model — stakes, democratic pay, no extraction — as we move toward trading.

The dream

A world where anyone, whatever their background, can build something real with technology — and own what they create.

What we're building

~1.5 million lines of code across four products in active development.

  • Confidentia

    EdTech — students rate their own confidence against GCSE and A-Level specs. Confidence, not judgement.

  • fynd.deals

    Deal aggregator for retail clothing — helping tight budgets stretch further.

  • AI Dev Kit

    Open-source framework and companion book for building software properly without a CS degree gate.

  • Starborn Legacy

    4X space strategy game — creative range and commercial upside for the team.

RMS Dev ships the products. RMS Compute is owned inference infrastructure in planning — wholesale AI for the cooperative, not a fifth consumer brand.

Who we will hire

We will hire developers and operators from refugee and disadvantaged communities, via progressive social initiatives — people the tech economy locks out.
