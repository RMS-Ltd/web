Ethos

Why we exist, how we intend to show up, and where we stand as a company today.

How we show up

RMS has its own design ethos at the umbrella level — distinct from the louder, product-specific branding each project will carry. We aim to feel like a laid-back, modern technology company: approachable, not stiff; capable, not performative.

  • Laid-back modern
  • Progressive
  • Socially aware
  • Environmentally aware
  • High-tech
Progressive
Worker ownership, democratic pay, hiring through progressive social initiatives — structure that matches the story we tell.
Socially aware
We build with and for people the tech economy locks out; inclusion is operational, not a slide in a deck.
Environmentally aware
Owned compute and careful infrastructure choices — especially under RMS Compute — so we are not careless about energy and footprint as we scale.
High-tech
Serious software and hardware ambition: a large product portfolio in development and inference infrastructure in planning.

Product sites (Confidentia, fynd.deals, and the rest) will express their own personality. RMS stays the calm, coherent parent brand.

Where we are

RMS Ltd. is incorporated and not trading. There is no trading finance structure yet — accounts and cooperative governance in full will follow when we start trading.

Right now the company is founder-led: one employee in practice, building the portfolio before we hire. We are applying the worker-owned cooperative model — stakes, democratic pay, no extraction — as we move toward trading.

The dream

A world where anyone, whatever their background, can build something real with technology — and own what they create.

How we work

Every employee will own a stake in the company. Every salary will be set democratically by the team — including the founder's. We are building for ownership and fair reward, not extraction.

RMS Ltd. is the umbrella; day-to-day product work runs through RMS Dev, platform and inference through RMS Compute. See Projects for how those fit together.

Who we will hire

We will hire developers and operators from refugee and disadvantaged communities, via progressive social initiatives — people the tech economy locks out.

Modern tech. Owned together.
