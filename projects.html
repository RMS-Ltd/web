Projects

How work is organised under the RMS Ltd. umbrella — arms first, then the product brands RMS Dev builds.

Umbrella vs product brands

This site is the RMS umbrella layer: legal entity, cooperative model, and structure — with a laid-back, modern, values-led tone (see how we show up). Each product (and RMS Compute when it trades) carries its own louder marketing and visual identity.

Under RMS Ltd.

  • RMS Dev

    Development arm — builds and ships product software.

    Confidentia, fynd.deals, AI Dev Kit, and Starborn Legacy are developed here. Each is its own brand; RMS Dev is the team and cost centre behind them.

  • RMS Compute

    Compute arm — owned inference and platform operations.

    Wholesale AI infrastructure for the cooperative (and eventually external customers). It is an arm, not a sibling consumer product like Confidentia — but it will have its own go-to-market identity when live, the way a product line would.

~1.5 million lines of code across the four Dev products in active development.
